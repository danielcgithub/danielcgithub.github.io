About Daniel

Daniel is a Software Engineer currently working as a Product Owner in LMI Ericsson.

Officially, the main part of Daniels job is to create a vision of what to build and convey that vision to the development team in a clear and simple manner.

That's just a simple way of saying that he loves to think of ideas to build, lead and drive the direction of projects and to work with teams to achieve goals in a lean and agile manner to continuously provide value to the organisation.
